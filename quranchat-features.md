# QuranChat: Your Spiritual Companion Powered by AI

QuranChat is a modern, premium mobile application designed to help Muslims build a meaningful, daily connection with the Holy Quran through advanced AI technology, guided journeys, and habit-building features.

### 1. **QuranChat AI: Your Spiritual Dialogue**
Engage with the Quran like never before. Our custom-trained AI assistant helps you explore and reflect with three specialized modes:
- **Verse Explorer**: Deep dive into the context, tafsir, and linguistic beauties of specific verses.
- **Spiritual Reflection**: Find guidance and emotional support through Quranic wisdom for your daily life.
- **Source Finder**: Simply describe a topic or emotion, and the AI finds relevant verses to guide you.
*Pro tip: The AI always cites its sources, allowing you to jump directly to the verse.*

### 2. **Guided Thematic Journeys**
Curated paths designed for your emotional and spiritual growth. Instead of reading randomly, follow a structured 7-14 day journey:
- **Finding Peace**: Verses to calm the heart and mind.
- **Cultivating Patience**: Wisdom for times of trial and waiting.
- **Grateful Heart**: Building a life of shukur and abundance.
- **Healing & Hope**: Spiritual medicine for the soul.

### 3. **Smart Habit Building (The Streak System)**
Stay motivated with our beautiful, gamified habit-tracking ecosystem:
- **Interactive Streaks**: A vibrant Lottie-powered celebration with haptic feedback for every day you read.
- **Daily Goals**: Set your target (verses/day) and watch your progress ring fill up in real-time.
- **Streak Protection**: Smart notifications that remind you exactly when your streak is about to break.
- **Detailed Analytics**: Visualize your spiritual growth with weekly and monthly charts.

### 4. **Intention-Based Onboarding**
A personalized start to your journey:
- **Set Your Purpose**: Choose your primary spiritual goal (Find Peace, Build Consistency, Seek Guidance, or Strengthen Faith) right from the start.
- **Tailored Experience**: The app adapts its voice and journey recommendations based on your initial intention.
