Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Quran Chat ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account and use our App, we collect:

  • • Email Address: For account creation, authentication, and password recovery
  • • Password: Stored in encrypted form for account security
  • • Display Name: Optional name you provide for your profile
  • • Bio: Optional biographical information

1.2 Reading Activity Data

To provide personalized features and track your progress:

  • • Verse Reading Progress: Which Quranic verses you have read
  • • Reading History: Dates and times when you read verses
  • • Bookmarks: Verses you save with optional personal notes
  • • Journey Progress: Your progress through guided reading journeys
  • • Daily Goals: Your customized daily reading targets
  • • AI Chat History: Messages exchanged with the AI assistant (stored purely for your reference and to maintain conversation context)

1.3 Usage Statistics

We automatically collect certain information about how you use the App:

  • • Reading Statistics: Daily streaks, total verses read, active days
  • • Feature Usage: Which app features you interact with
  • • Device Information: Device type, operating system version (for compatibility)

1.4 Location Data (Optional)

If you enable prayer times features, we collect location data (city or coordinates) solely for calculating accurate prayer times. Location data is stored only for this purpose.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core App Functionality

  • • Authenticate your account and maintain login sessions
  • • Display your reading progress, streaks, and achievements
  • • Save and sync your bookmarks, notes, and journey progress
  • • Provide personalized reading statistics and insights
  • • Calculate and display prayer times based on your location

2.2 Service Improvement

  • • Analyze usage patterns to improve app features
  • • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • • Optimize app performance

2.3 Communication

  • • Send password reset emails when requested
  • • Provide important service updates or security notices
  • • Respond to your support requests

2.4 Notifications

We send in-app notifications for achievement milestones, journey completions, and daily goal alerts. All notifications are generated locally and do not involve third-party services.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud-based backend service (PostgreSQL database) located in secure, industry-standard facilities. All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS.

3.2 Security Measures

  • • Password Security: Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • • Database Security: Row-level security (RLS) policies ensure users can only access their own data
  • • Secure APIs: All API communications use HTTPS encryption
  • • Regular Updates: We regularly update our security protocols

3.3 Data Retention

  • • Active Accounts: Data is retained while your account is active
  • • Deleted Accounts: All data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • • Backups: Backup copies are maintained for disaster recovery and are deleted after 90 days

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.2 Third-Party Services We Use

Supabase (Backend Infrastructure): Database, authentication, and backend services. Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

Quran.com API (Verse Data): Fetch Quranic verses, translations, and audio. No personal information is shared. Privacy Policy: https://quran.com/privacy

OpenAI (AI Analysis): We use OpenAI to power our AI Chat features. Your message content is shared with OpenAI solely to generate responses. We do not share your email, name, or other identity details with OpenAI. Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/privacy

RevenueCat (In-App Purchases): We use RevenueCat to process subscriptions and verify your premium status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous identifier and purchase history. They do not receive your payment details (which are handled by Apple). Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

4.3 When We May Disclose Information

  • • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • • Safety: To protect the safety, rights, or property of users or the public
  • • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger or acquisition (users will be notified)
  • • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize disclosure

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • • Access: View all your personal data in the App (via Profile and Settings)
  • • Update: Edit your name, bio, and preferences at any time
  • • Export: Request a copy of your data (contact support@quranapp.com)
  • • Delete: Permanently delete your account via Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account

5.2 Regional Privacy Rights

For EU Users (GDPR): Right to data portability, right to be forgotten, right to restrict processing, right to object to processing, right to lodge a complaint with supervisory authority.

For California Users (CCPA): Right to know what personal information is collected, right to delete personal information, right to opt-out of sale (we don't sell data), right to non-discrimination.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is intended for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If we discover we have collected information from a child under 13 without verification of parental consent, we will delete that information immediately.

7. Data Sharing Features

  • • Journey Achievements: Share completed journeys on social media (optional)

  • • Notes: Your notes are private by default and never shared without your explicit action

Unless you explicitly choose to share, your reading progress, bookmarks, notes, and statistics remain completely private.

8. Cookies and Tracking

What We DON'T Use: No advertising cookies, no third-party tracking pixels, no behavioral advertising networks, no cross-site tracking.
