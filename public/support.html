Frequently Asked Questions

Account & login

  • Forgot password? Use “Forgot password” on the login screen. We’ll send a reset link to your email.
  • Can’t sign in? Check that you’re using the correct email and that your password is entered correctly. If it still fails, contact us at the email above.
  • Delete my account? Open the app → Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account. This permanently removes your data.

Using the app

  • Reading progress not saving? Make sure you’re logged in and have a stable internet connection. Progress is synced to your account.
  • Prayer times wrong? Prayer times depend on your location. Enable location in Settings (or enter your city) so we can calculate them correctly.
  • AI chat not responding? Check your internet connection. If the issue continues, try closing and reopening the app, or contact us.

Technical issues

  • App crashes or freezes? Try closing the app fully and reopening it. If it keeps happening, tell us your device model and iOS version so we can investigate.
  • Content not loading? Check your Wi‑Fi or mobile data. If other apps work but Quran Chat doesn’t, email us with details.
  • Something else? Describe what you see (and any error message) and send it to the email above. We’ll look into it.
